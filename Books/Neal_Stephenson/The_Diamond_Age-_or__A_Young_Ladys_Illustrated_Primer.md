## Review

Perhaps his best book? Better than [b:Snow Crash|40651883|Snow Crash|Neal Stephenson|https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/books/1530057753l/40651883._SX50_.jpg|493634]?
